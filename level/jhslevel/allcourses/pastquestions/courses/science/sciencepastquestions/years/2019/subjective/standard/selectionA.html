Suggested Answer

1. (a) (i) Temperature = 100°C or 212°F or 373K

(ii) Observations (Nails) Nails 1, 2, 3 and 4 will drop off one after the other - Nail 1 drops first, nail 2 second, nail 3 third and nail 4 fourth

(iii) Observations(Thermometers) The readings of Thermometers A,B, C and D will increase progressively till they reach 100° C -Thermometer A reaches 100° C first, B second,C third and D fourth

(iv) Mode of heat transfer - Conduction

(v) Effects of heat
1. Causes increase in temperature(as shown by the thermometer readings)
2. Causes change in state (candle wax melts - changes from solid to liquid)

(vi) Aim of experiment
1. To show that metals are good conductors of heat
2. To show that heat travels through metals
